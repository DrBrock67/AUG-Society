The AUGazoic Era

Fall 1983 - PHARC formed

Circa 1988 to 1989 - Transmogrification of PHARC ashes into phoenix of AUG

 

1990 - The "Golden" Year

May 1990 - Zippo perishes at Indy – a turn for the worse to be sure

Summer 1991 - Emergence of Zoso (great-grandnephew of Zippo)

Fall 1991 - Zoso proposes AUGFest II at Laguna Madre/Lake Falcon

Winter 1991 - Massive preparations for AUGFest II (Pace Bend)

Spring 1992 - Failure of AUGFest II; lamentations of the forefathers

 

1993 - The "Dead" Year

Circa 1993 - Mick expunged from AUG

Circa 1993 - Gorbag evidently alive – yet still unavailable

Summer 1994 - Much Maligned (AUG approved) Ranger Blast; "Big No" Nagy vies for membership, but is denied

December 1994 - Hugely Maligned (AUG approved) Boyz Blast in the Big Easy; tix on the "4 to 5 yard line"

Winter 1994 - Zoso disemboweled by Antarctic polar bear (expired)

 

1995 - The "Quickening"

Spring 1995 - Birth of Deleterious Jones & Neon Rodman

March 1995 - Wango’s call to arms

May 1995 - AUGFest II – Johnson Memorial, AusTex; Ferret inAUGurated

October 1996 - Flathead, Ferret, & Rewd witness 1st ever Ranger home playoff game (Yanks insert pud in Rump Rangers)

1998 - The "Missing" Year (Da ‘Boyz begin to suck; AUG begins to swallow)

 

1999 The "Millennial Re-birth"

Fall 1999 - AUGFest III (aka AUG2K) proposed by Neon Rodman

December 1999 - Deleterious Jones launches ambitious AUG website

New Year’s Day 2000 – AUG world domination eminent

May 2000 - AUG 2K ("Drinking Pee in D.C.") plans finalized & flights booked

June 2000 - AUG 2K – Washington, DC

September 2000 - AUGFest Texas, semi-sanctioned AUG event at the House of Ferret (Wango & Rewd in the house)

April 2001 - AUG Website update; plans for AUGFest "AUGht 1" in Corpus Christi commence
