5 March 1995

A.U.G. (need more be said?)

UP from the ashes, like a set of Barreta keys, a foreboding band of myth monsters has arisen. I commend thee wanGO!! The wit and wisdom displayed in your recent call to arms has caused my loins to shrink in terror, what must the future hold for the faint of heart. Still, it is with confidence I brace myself for the cold night to come, for I know that I am chosen: I am righteous: I have paid Charon midstream and still seen both sides of the river Styx, 1 have climbed X-2, 1've lost my train of thought... Oh. Yes - I am a charter Augster, and I shalt know no fear.

This cry for the lost of our souls could not have tarried longer without disastrous consequences. We Indeed are near the end. 7he Lord shall smite us mightily, He shall come like a level 12, +4 leather armour clad thief in the night. Believe me, brethren. If you've got ‘em, smoke ‘em. As Viv’s husband Tom once said,

"Who is the third. Who walks always beside you?

When 1 count, there are only you and I together

But when I look ahead up the white road

There is always another one walking beside you

Gliding wrapt in a brown mantle, hooded

I do not Know whether a man or woman

-But who is that on the other side of you?"

-Well, it ain't yeoman purser Gopher; it's the All-mighty fellows, so take heed. We walk in the unreal city. -We are mere denizens of The Wasteland.

While I commend the battle cry which still rings, ooo-WAH! in My ears, 1 must be the first to condemn as well. Another great poet once said, "And 1, 1 took the road less traveled by, and that has made a markedly more significant than merely appreciable difference." So what is the Point? 1'm getting there you impatient sonsabitchs! Here it is...

Take flight from the cities you cowardly, urban gutter-siphonlng, filth mongers! Big D? Big A? Big A-wipe is more like it. 7he BallPark is a Mecca: no, it is Mecca. And in the words of a horribly putrid mortal Dan Cortez, "Been there, done that shit," And I don't think I need to remind anyone - it sucked! No beer after midnight, no babes atol, no slam-fest, just wimped out, whiny assed, hamster loving, unpoignant, flimsy gab.

No offense wanGO.

But it's about survival! No Leda in the city, Bubba. Who needs candles when there's so much footcandle light pollution that the cfc's are aglow all night? If you head to the woods, you will have enough beer. Why? Because you have to. The city makes you weak. You become passive, believing everything is within arm's reach. And what happens when the remote disappears? Our families find us in the early morning white noise glow of our televisions, blubbering like babies. " I can't find ESPN2." Pitiful.

Furthermore, I am not buying another plane, train or bus ticket. And I am not driving nine straight hours. And, 1 am certainly not doing it to go straight to the cosmic hellhole of the skyscraper veneered Metroplex. 1 will go there on two conditions. (i) you get Harold Taft to personally tattoo an occluded front on my forehead, and (ii) you guarantee that Gaylord Perry will be the starting pitcher for the Texscab Rangers.

Besides, you can't hack on the freeway. You could die and lose your hack, too. It's time to go back to our roots. Big-ass fire, alphabet tent sex, 12-gauge wire - that's what little augs are made of. The city is a cell of desolation, an eventual morgue. As children we longed for a ticket to the citv, a merry-go-round of bright lights and opportunity. -We were fools grasping at gold rings which inevitably were brass.

"If we seek solace in the prisons of the distant past

Security in human systems we’re told will always

always last.

Emotions are the sail and blind faith the mast

"Without the breath of real freedom we're getttng

nowhere fast"

- fugg'n Sting 'n shit, man

'Nuff said. Your options, my options, are:

Lake Travis

Lake Buchanan

Lake Canyon

Lake Whitman (perhaps, big perhaps)

I had a dream. A fishhook is lodged in my neck. A blonde psychologist winds two strands of six gauge wire through the eyelet and yanks. An aspiring med student sutures my carotid artery with legit 12 gauge wire. Turning to the cranium minimizer I say, "I should be fuckin' dead now, my friend! -We just witnessed a miracle, and I want you to fuckin' acknowledge it!" 7he scruffy shrink says, "Okay man, it was a miracle, can we leave now?"

 

Hold fast to dreams, my friends, for if dreams die, life is a broken winged penguin, that couldn't fly to begin with, but at least his flipper didn't hurt so goddam much.

Zofo Lives!!

 

Neon Rodman
