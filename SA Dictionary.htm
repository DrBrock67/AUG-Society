A Modest Attempt at an SA Dictionary

 

 

CORE 

Dominant - Depending on the enthusiasm of the speaker, anything from mildly cool to incredibly cool.

Souflex - Variant of dominant, a wrestling term, used more often to describe something surprisingly cool or cool beyond reasonable expectations given the subject.

Cheezy - Lame, uncool. Also used as a noun to describe someone's shasta (i.e. cheese).

Beware the variants Cheezy soulflex - surprisingly or extraordinarily uncool.

Dude - The basic pronoun, nongender-specific greeting, question, or exclamation of disdain, surprise or disgust. Very versatile.

Wahoo - to acquire by theft.
