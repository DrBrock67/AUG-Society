The Blasphemeatitudes of Wango

 

On the call to arms for Cinco de AUG-o

There followed two nasty beatitudes

Their yearnings did beckon to what would follow

For the dealings of all those fine AUG dudes….

 

 

The rewD’s Prayer

Our Wango,
whose response was given,
hallowed be thy game.
AUGfest will come,
we'll have some fun,
in Texas, damn close to heaven.
Render us this day a gamely stead
and allow us our misgivings
as we portend to misgive those against us.
And lead us not into meandering,
but deliver us from piffle,
for thine is the ersatz,
and the impotence,
and the gory,
forever
and ever.
Hey Man!

 

 

The ROdARY

Hail, Wango, full of haste.
The horde is pithy.
Wretched art thou among augsters
and heinous are your fruit of the looms -- undies.
Only, Wango, brother of Rod,
tip with us sinners now and with the sourness of our breath

– hymen
