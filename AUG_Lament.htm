Augmentation Lamentation

What awful, drunken mercenaries these,
Who strive to fecundate the world with guile.


They show sophistication in their pleas,
While otherwise their countenance is vile.


Can these modern charlatans convince
a host of maidens that they mean to dance


through night and into morn as though a prince
while seeking only minutes in their pants?


Though niceties and compliments sound true,
these hooligans cannot by means prevail


in winning precious hearts of virgins few
when all the lot should find themselves in jail.


They search for one last grand orgasmic stand
A charnel house would better suit this band.
