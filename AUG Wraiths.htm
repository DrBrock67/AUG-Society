AUG Wraiths

When ashen faces scan the forest road,
they find their darkest fears becoming real.
Their skin becomes as frigid as a toad's.
Soon, they know, pure anguish they will feel.

The sweat pours down their brows in rivulets,
as quiet woodland creatures cringe with fright.
The fearful cling to heirloom amulets
they pray will guard their lives against the night.

The riders come -- a thunderous shrieking horde.
Their wails and clanging arms make ghostly sound.
A banshee army wiping from the board
all fools who vainly strive to hold their ground.

The doors are barred against the sudden spate
of misanthropic beasts who curse all fate
