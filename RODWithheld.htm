Withheld

One cannot fathom my reserve these days

I know lead ever onward toward this end --

A horrible an deviant charade,

wherein our troupe becomes a set of fiends.

I walk through normal life as though we don't

tranform ourselves into wrecking creue.

Deluded ... contemplating that we won't

wreak havoc on an unsuspecting few.

My destiny is cast I recognize.

I cannot keep my truer self at bay.

My waking life seems somehow full of lies.

The fiction's what I do on normal days.

I manifest myself as warm of heart;

although, I know when called I'll play my part.
