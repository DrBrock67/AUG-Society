Primary Objective: Become the last player standing with less than 10 points.

Secondary Objective: Become the last player standing.

Primary Scoring: 0 - 10 (Get to 10, yerrr out!)

Secondary Scoring: If you can fit it in, go for it.

No. of Players: 2 - 20

No. of Good Plrs.: 0

Order of Play

A rotation is established among the players which must be enforced by the soberest or brainiest participant. The first player in the rotation shoots. The next player in sequence will:

(1) if the shot is made, attempt to duplicate it;

(2) if the shot is missed, attempt to tip the ball in.

In attempting to tip, the player must be airborne; otherwise, play terminates. If the second man tips and misses, the next man in rotation attempts to tip, and so on, and so on, and so on, until you have a dead ball, either through successful tippage, footfault, player fucks the rotation, or ball to ground contact.

If the first shot is made and the second man misses, tip resumes as described above, and the errant shooter receives a point. If man two is successful, shooter one receives a point, and the next man in rotation takes a shot. If any player shoots an airball (i.e. no rim contact), he receives a point. Faulty tips do not count as airballs.

Any successful tip (other than a slam) results in a point against the last player to touch the ball. Any tip resulting in a dunk results in two points against the last player to touch the ball. Any player leaving the game with I 0 points (or more, for really hapless, dare I even call them tipsters) vanishes from the rotation as though they never existed. The last player with less than 10 points is the victor, a.k.a. Baglick Magnifico, or B.M..
