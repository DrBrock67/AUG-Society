AUG Definitions

 

 

AUGean (adjective) – extremely formidable or difficult and occasionally distasteful.

 

AUGeas (noun) – King of Elis, his stable, neglected for 30 years, had to be cleaned by Hercules

 

AUGer (noun) – a tool having a helical shaft or member used for boring holes.

 

AUGht (pronoun) – anything, everything.

 

AUGht (noun, archaic) – non-entity, nothing, zero.

 

AUGite (noun) – an aluminous black or dark green pyroxene found in igneous rocks.

 

AUGmentative (adjective) – indicating large size and awkwardness or unattractiveness.

 

AUGratin (adjective) – covered with bread crumbs or grated cheese and broiled.

 

AUGur (noun) – one held to foretell events by omens.

 

AUGust (adjective) – marked by majestic dignity or grandeur.
